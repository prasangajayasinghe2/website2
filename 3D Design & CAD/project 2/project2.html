Project Overview
A detailed 3D ship model designed for scale model production. The project focused on accurately recreating the hull geometry, superstructure, and deck features using surface modelling techniques in SolidWorks.
Key challenges included maintaining surface continuity across compound curves of the hull and ensuring all proportions were true to scale. The final model was prepared with clean geometry suitable for 3D printing or physical model manufacturing.
